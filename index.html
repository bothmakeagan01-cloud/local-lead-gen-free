üí° Free Game: 5 Meta Ad Mistakes Killing Your ROI

Mistake #1: Broad Targeting Without Exclusions

Most businesses target everyone in their city. Smart move? Target within 5-7 miles of your location and EXCLUDE people who already like your page. You'll cut costs by 40% instantly.

Mistake #2: Not Using Dynamic Creative Testing

Upload 3-5 images, 3-5 headlines, and let Meta's AI find the winning combination. We've seen CTR improve by 250% just from this one change.

Mistake #3: Ignoring Your Pixel Data

Install the Meta Pixel, set up Conversions API, and track EVERYTHING. Most businesses are flying blind. With proper tracking, you'll know exactly which ads drive revenue.

Mistake #4: Manual Follow-Up Instead of Automation

78% of customers buy from the business that responds first. Set up instant SMS responses and you'll book 3x more appointments. We use GHL workflows that text leads within 60 seconds.

Mistake #5: One Campaign Wonder Syndrome

Running one campaign? You're leaving money on the table. Run 3 campaigns minimum: Cold traffic for new customers, retargeting for website visitors, and a loyalty campaign for past customers.
